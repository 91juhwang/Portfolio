About Me

Hi! My name is James Hwang, and I am a web-developer residing in New York.

I was first amazed by the power of technology, then inspired by the infinite possibilities of programming. My life became so much more fulfilled after learning to program because I felt that I finally found something that I enjoy.

I am a graduate of web-development intensive at New York Code + Design Academy. I have a B.A. in Economics and previously worked at a digital agency, where I gained professional experience as a Strategy/Product Planner. I also helped Korean businesses and job-seekers to succeed in U.S. markets as a consultant at a Korean government agency. Because of my diverse background, I am able to understand both the planning and the development sides of business. I am devoted to continuing on my learning to contribute to the great ideas and visions.

I am an outgoing, positive person who loves to spend time with my family and friends. I love traveling, bowling, playing ping-pong, eating, camping, skiing, gaming, learning, and many more fun things in life. I am always interested in fun projects, so please shoot me an email to say hello!
