Hi! My name is James Hwang, and I am a web-developer residing in New York.

I was first amazed by the power of technology, then inspired by the infinite possibilities of programming. My life became so much fulfilling after learning to program because I feel that I finally found something that I enjoy.

I am a graduate of web-development intensive programming bootcamp at New York Code + Design Academy. I have a B.A. in economics and previously worked in a digital advertising agency, where I gained professional experiences as a strategy/product planner. I worked as a consultant at a Korean government agency, and helped Korean businesses and job-seekers to succeed in U.S. markets. Because of my various background, I am able to understand both planning and the development sides of the business, and I am devoted to continue on my learning to contribute to the great ideas and visions.

I am an outgoing, positive person who loves to spend time with my friends. I love traveling, bowling, playing ping-pong, eatting, camping, skiing, gaming, partying, learning, and many more fun things in our life.

I am always interested in fun projects, so please shoot me an email to say hello!
