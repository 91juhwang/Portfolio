SAY HELLO

=

hello.junsuk@gmail.com

Hi! My name is James Hwang, and I am a web-developer residing in Queens, New York.

My interest lies in the digital world of web-development and digital marketing. I was always curious about vast genres of the digital world, which led me to complete an intensive web-development program at New York Code + Design Academy.

I have a B.A. in economics and previously worked in a digital advertising agency, where I gained professional experiences as a strategy/product planner. I worked as a consultant at a Korean government agency,and helped Korean businesses and job-seekers to succeed in U.S. markets. Due to my background and natural aptitude, I understand both development and the business aspects, which I am continue to my learning and improvements.

Beside my professional experience, I often look for volunteer opportunities to give back and show an appreciation for things I sometimes take for granted. I taught Korean/American language and culture to those who were interested, and volunteered to serve short-term services like home repair, street cleaning and children education. I am also a founder of a Facebook group, Stony Brook Forever, which connects and brings more than 2,100 members together affiliated with Stony Brook University.

I am always open to opportunities with good cause and vision, so please shoot me an email to say hello!
